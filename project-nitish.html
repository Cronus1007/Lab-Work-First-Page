Objectives of the Course


Summer School is a cumulative initiative of all the prestige technical institutions of the country including IITs,NITs, IIITs and GFTIs for transforming the landscape of technical and desicion making system via Artificial Intelligence(AI) and Data Science(DS). Recent advances in Machine Learning and deep-learning, key components of AI..The aim of the course is to bring together the researchers and practitioners in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science to discuss new foundational theoretical results,methods and applications. On the side of theory and methods, this includes both novel Machine Learning and Statistical Approaches to data-science with a focus on algorithmic and mathematical foundations.On the side of applications, this includes important challenges faced by different industries in addressing real-world data science problems including in the Enviornment and Health-Care domains. The workshop will enable cross-fertilization of ideas and the development of further research capacity. The goal is to discuss and identify important challenges that need to be addressed, both in theory and practice for further advancement of using AI and DS in solving socio-economic problems.
